About Us

Prime Power Traders, under the visionary leadership of CEO Mushtaq Ahmad, has been a cornerstone in the industrial machinery trading sector since its establishment in 2005. Operating as a proprietorship, we specialize in sourcing and delivering top-notch industrial equipment, including a diverse range of industrial chillers, compressors, generators, and motors. Mushtaq Ahmad, as the founder, personally travels to countries such as the UK, Germany, China, and Dubai, ensuring that we procure the highest quality machinery at competitive prices.

Our commitment to excellence goes beyond transactions; it's a commitment to providing quality products and unmatched service. We operate with a unique model, offering a personalized experience to clients who visit our interconnected warehouses in Darogwala. With a total of three attached warehouses, we showcase our machinery, ensuring clients have direct access to information and a firsthand look at our offerings.

As an engineer-led company, we pride ourselves on not only providing detailed information and handling paperwork but also offering expert repair services. From fixing programmable logic controllers (PLCs) to addressing hardware issues, we stand by our products with reliable after-sales support. Our mission at Prime Power Traders is clear: to provide quality machinery and unparalleled service to our clients. This commitment is further emphasized by our provision of warranties on select products. Over the years, we have built a legacy of integrity, expertise, and customer satisfaction, making Prime Power Traders your trusted partner in industrial machinery solutions.
